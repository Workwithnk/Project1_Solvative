what actually PeerFives is?

PeerFives is a peer-to-peer employee recognition program that helps to connect a growing team. It gives the points as a reward in which all users can individually share their points and based on the points they can claim their rewards.

Concept of peerFives

PeerFives Reward system gives you a complete way to appriciate the smart employees in your team by providing them PeerFives Reward points .

How it works ?

Both peers (both person) can give individually peerFives points to each other , The peerFives points transaction can done at any moment (any time) , when they wants.

Transaction done with

  1. Individual : peer to peer
  2. Team Level : leaders to members
  3. Organisation Level : top to bottom

Method for claiming rewards

The user just has to visit the online store of peerFive and there they can do shopping like normal shopping with other stores (e. g Amazon). Here they will use their peerFives points for shopping.
PeerFives has done collaboration with Amazon Flipkart , Domino's , Swiggy and many more merchandise.
